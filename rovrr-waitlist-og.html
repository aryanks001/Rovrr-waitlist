What I Am Building

SCOUT. GUIDE. PREPARE.

Three problems I lived personally. One platform I am building to solve all three. Here is what it looks like.

01 — SCOUT
Find Jobs Nobody Else Sees

I am building a system that monitors LinkedIn feed posts where hiring managers announce roles before posting anywhere else. Instagram business accounts. Telegram channels used by startup communities. Company career pages directly. All verified. All within 24 hours. Before anyone else finds them.

Verified · 24 Hour Window
02 — GUIDE
Your Complete Application in 60 Seconds

For every role you want, the goal is to generate your complete application automatically. An ATS optimized resume tailored to that specific role. A cover letter written for the hiring manager. A direct recruiter outreach email ready to send. Not templates. Built for this job. This company. Right now.

ATS Optimized · Per Role
03 — PREPARE
Walk In Knowing What is Coming

The interview prep module will read the job description, analyse the company, and simulate the actual interview before it happens. Specific questions. Specific context. Specific hiring manager patterns. So you walk in knowing exactly what this conversation will look like. No more underconfidence.

AI Coach · Role Specific
